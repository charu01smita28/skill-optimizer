EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT — Helix Bio Sciences (excerpt)

Section 4. Inventions and Work Product
Employee hereby assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to any inventions, discoveries, improvements, original works of authorship, designs, formulas, processes, software, mask works, trade secrets, and other intellectual property, whether patentable or copyrightable, that Employee, alone or jointly with others, conceives, develops, or reduces to practice during the term of employment, whether or not during working hours and whether or not using Company resources, that relate in any way to the Company's business, research, or development.

Section 7. Confidentiality
Employee shall hold all Confidential Information of the Company in strict confidence, both during and indefinitely after the term of employment. "Confidential Information" includes, without limitation, all non-public information of any kind that Employee learns in the course of employment.

Section 9. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation
For a period of twelve (12) months following termination of employment for any reason, Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, (a) engage in any business that competes with the Company in any geography in which the Company operates, or (b) solicit any Company employee, contractor, customer, or prospective customer with whom Employee had material contact during the final twenty-four (24) months of employment.
