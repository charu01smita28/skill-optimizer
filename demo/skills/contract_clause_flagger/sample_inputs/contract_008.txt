Section 6. Indemnification
Reseller shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Provider against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of (a) Reseller's marketing or sale of the Services, (b) any representations made by Reseller about the Services beyond those expressly authorized by Provider in writing, and (c) Reseller's breach of this Agreement. Provider's indemnification of Reseller is limited to direct third-party claims of intellectual property infringement and is capped at the commissions paid to Reseller in the prior twelve (12) months.

Section 12. Term and Termination
This Agreement has an initial term of twenty-four (24) months and renews automatically for successive twelve-month terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least one hundred twenty (120) days prior to the end of the then-current term.
