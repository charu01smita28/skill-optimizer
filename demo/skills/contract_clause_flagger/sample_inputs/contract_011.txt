CO-MARKETING AGREEMENT — Borealis Networks & Partner

Section 3. Use of Marks
Each party grants the other a non-exclusive, royalty-free, revocable license to use its trademarks, logos, and brand assets ("Marks") solely for the purposes of (a) joint marketing materials approved in writing by both parties, and (b) reference to the existence of this commercial relationship. All goodwill arising from such use shall inure to the benefit of the Mark-owning party.

Section 5. Approval Process
All co-branded materials, including press releases, blog posts, case studies, conference presentations, and customer-facing content, must be reviewed and approved in writing by both parties prior to publication. Either party may withhold approval in its reasonable discretion.

Section 7. Indemnification of Claims
Each party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims arising out of (i) factually false or misleading statements made by the indemnifying party in any co-marketing material, or (ii) the indemnifying party's unauthorized use of the other party's Marks beyond the scope of the license granted herein.

Section 9. Term
This Agreement shall remain in effect until terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days' written notice. There is no automatic renewal mechanism.
