Section 3. Term and Renewal
The initial Subscription Term is twelve (12) months. The Agreement shall not automatically renew. Any renewal requires a new signed Order Form executed by both parties no later than the end of the then-current Term.

Section 7. Mutual Limitation of Liability
Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed two (2) times the fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to liability. The foregoing cap does not apply to (i) liability for gross negligence, willful misconduct, or fraud, (ii) breach of confidentiality obligations, or (iii) Provider's indemnification obligations under Section 9.

Section 9. Indemnification
Provider shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Customer against any third-party claim that the Services infringe any patent, copyright, trademark, or trade secret. Provider's obligation is conditioned on prompt written notice from Customer and reasonable cooperation in defense.

Section 12. Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement at the end of the then-current Subscription Term upon thirty (30) days' written notice. No early-termination penalties apply to either party.
