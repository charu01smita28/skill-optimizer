EQUIPMENT LEASE AGREEMENT — Pacific Rail Holdings

Section 4. Term and Early Termination
The Lease commences on the Delivery Date and continues for sixty (60) consecutive months. Lessee may not terminate this Lease prior to the end of the term. In the event Lessee attempts to terminate early, returns the Equipment, or ceases payment, Lessor shall be entitled to immediately accelerate and collect all remaining rental payments through the end of the term, plus the residual value of the Equipment as set forth in Schedule C, plus reasonable repossession and remarketing costs.

Section 6. Insurance
Lessee shall, at Lessee's sole cost and expense, maintain (i) all-risk property insurance covering the Equipment for its full replacement value, and (ii) commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than five million U.S. dollars (USD 5,000,000) per occurrence, in each case naming Lessor as additional insured and loss payee.

Section 8. Indemnification
Lessee shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lessor from any and all claims, damages, fines, penalties, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of the use, operation, possession, condition, or maintenance of the Equipment during the term, regardless of the cause and regardless of whether such claims arise in whole or in part from Lessor's negligence (excepting only Lessor's gross negligence or willful misconduct).

Section 11. Title
Title to the Equipment shall remain with Lessor at all times. Nothing in this Lease shall be construed to grant Lessee any ownership interest, security interest, or right of purchase in the Equipment.
