AFFILIATE / REFERRAL PARTNER AGREEMENT — Chartreuse Marketing Group

Section 3. Referral Fees
Provider shall pay Affiliate a referral fee equal to ten percent (10%) of the net subscription revenue actually collected by Provider from Qualified Referrals during the first twelve (12) months of each such customer's initial Subscription Term. Referral fees are paid quarterly within forty-five (45) days after the close of each calendar quarter.

Section 5. Qualified Referral
A "Qualified Referral" means a prospective customer (i) introduced to Provider by Affiliate via the agreed referral form, (ii) not previously known to Provider as a sales lead, (iii) that executes a paid subscription within one hundred twenty (120) days of the introduction. Provider's CRM record shall be the conclusive evidence as to whether a referral qualifies.

Section 7. Non-Solicitation of Customers
During the term of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months thereafter, Affiliate shall not directly or indirectly solicit any customer of Provider (whether or not such customer was introduced by Affiliate) on behalf of any competing product or service.

Section 9. No Authority
Affiliate is an independent contractor. Affiliate has no authority to bind Provider, make representations on Provider's behalf beyond marketing materials supplied by Provider, or set or modify pricing.
