MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

A. TERM. The Agreement runs for a fixed term of two (2) years. Renewal requires mutual written agreement at least sixty (60) days before expiration.

B. INDEMNIFICATION. Each party shall indemnify the other for third-party claims of (i) gross negligence or willful misconduct, and (ii) infringement of the indemnifying party's IP, capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve (12) months.

C. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. Provider retains all rights to its pre-existing Services and platform IP. Customer retains all rights to its Customer Data and any custom configurations or integrations Customer creates using the Services.

D. CONFIDENTIALITY. Each party shall protect the other's confidential information for three (3) years following disclosure, using the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information.
